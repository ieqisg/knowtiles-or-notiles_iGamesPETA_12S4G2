Know Tiles or No Tiles Game Mechanics

The game can be played individually or as a group collaborating in answering all the questions.

How to Play

  1. From the Home Page, you will see two buttons: “Play” and “How to Play”.
  2. Click on “How to Play” to read the game mechanics.
  3. After reading the game mechanics, you can now start playing the game by clicking “Play.”
  4. There are 4 options from which you will choose among the 4 subjects – Gen. Biology, Gen. Chemistry, Gen. Physics, and Basic Calculus.
  5. After you click a subject, you will be presented with 9 tiles with a point system as follows:
Tile Type Points
Easy 100
Bonus question for Easy 150
Average 200
Bonus question for Average 250
Difficult 300
Bonus question for Difficult 350
  1. Choose a tile that you want to answer. There are no time limits in answering the questions or completing the game.
  2. After you choose a tile to answer, the question and choices will appear. Some questions do not have choices, and you must input your answer.
  3. Read the question and choices carefully and choose the best answer.
  4. The maximum point per subject is 1950, while the lowest possible point is a negative point. When the player reaches a negative point, the game can no longer keep going and the player must try again.
  5. If the answer is correct, a "CORRECT ANSWER" will appear with a continue button below. However, if the answer is incorrect, an “INCORRECT ANSWER" will appear with the correct answer to the question.
  6. When the player gets a correct answer, the points will be added, while if it is incorrect, the points will be deducted.
  7. If the player fails to get the correct answer in one question or tile, the player should select another tile or question and not choose the same one.
  8. Continue playing the game until you finish answering all 9 tiles. You must finish the 9 tiles or questions before you move on to the next subject.
  9. Once you finish, click the home button at the top left of the box
  10. Answer all 9 tiles in every subject to finish the entire game.

    Rules in Answering:

    Multiple Choices Questions:

    • Click the answer that best describes the question.

    Identification Questions:

    • Wrong spelling wrong.

    • The first letter is always capitalized.

    • Do not put a comma in numbers; a point is required only if it is a decimal number. (e.g., 2345.67)
